Project information

After logging in to Spotify, users may use this application to search for music. If the music exists, the search result will offer the user with all necessary information about it, including a Spotify link to the song and, if available, a 30-second preview. You may receive a list of concerts linked to the artist of the song by pressing the "Cerca Concerti" button, which includes information on the city, date, and location of the event. When you click on the location, a map with a marker showing the event's location appears. On the concert page, you can always use a private or public chat service, which was established with the purpose of allowing users to interact with other users who have reached the page to agree on the parameters of a concert or simply to organise themselves to attend the concert together.
